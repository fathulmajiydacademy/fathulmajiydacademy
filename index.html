Our History

Fathul Majiyd Academy was established in 2010 with the mission of providing quality education and moral upbringing for young minds. Over the years, we have grown into a reputable institution, known for academic excellence and holistic development of our students. Our vision is to nurture future leaders who are grounded in knowledge, values, and community service.
